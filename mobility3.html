3 - Conclusion



I do not regret a second of my stay in Ireland.
I meet countless wonderful people who have helped me along my journey to understand a little more about the world around me.
And even if there were many difficulties to overcome such as understanding the project, my medium spoken English at the time or even the search for accommodation, I really feel like this internship brought me a lot especially in maturity, computer science knowledge and autonomy.

I love this country and I will definitely go back.
