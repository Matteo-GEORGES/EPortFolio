Interview n°2

Person interviewed : Mr Alexandre BURON



Job : Developer team leader + my internship supervisor


Summary of the interview:

While being a team leader, you don't need to be perfect on the
technical aspect of your team work such as coding because it's their
work. Instead, you need to shine by your leadership skills such as
motivation, time management, communication or Conflict resolution.
It is very hard (even impossible) to be perfect on every of these topics
because there are so much. The principal key is the fast adaptation.
Being able to know what your team needs at any moment and be
ready to fulfill this need anytime, like a cook a Saturday evening.
Managing developers is finding the right balance between patience
and taking action.
